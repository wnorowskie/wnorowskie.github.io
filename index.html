    Vassar College & LACOL Schools

    In 2019 I graduated from the college prepatory Christian Brothers Academy in Lincroft, New Jersey. I was accepted to Vassar College with a 1490 SAT after receiving an athletic position offer. After trying a variety of courses I began to fall in love with Computer Science classes. I always enjoyed math and science but the problem solving nature of Computer Science really sparked my interest.

    Four years later I was a four-year starter, captain, and two time all-conference soccer player while maintaining a 3.7 GPA in a competitive major at a top liberal arts institution. In 2022 I had the opportunity to intern with the Data Science & Engineering team at ConstructConnect where I was given the responsibility and freedom to work on exciting projects and get hands-on data science experience. I decided to use my final year of athletic eligibility to continue my education and accepted a scholarship offer from the College of Charleston Men's Soccer staff. I am currently continuing my degree while I search for full-time employment.
